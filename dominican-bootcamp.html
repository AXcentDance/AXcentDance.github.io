Frequently Asked Questions

Is it only Dominican Bachata? +
Yes! This event focuses exclusively on authentic Dominican Bachata technique, musicality, and footwork.
Is it for every level? +
No, this bootcamp is designed for dancers with at least 1 year of Bachata experience. It is not suitable for absolute beginners.

Yes, you can transfer your ticket to another person. Please email us with the new attendee's name so we can update our guest list.
