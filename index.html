Our Pillars

01

Connection

+

It is more than just a dance class; it is about finding your tribe. Join a welcoming community where you can make friends, find new connections and grow.

02

Curriculum

+

First Bachata, then Sensual Foundation. We believe in a solid progression: learn the rules, master the rules, then break the rules. Build your unique style on a base of strong technique and authentic roots.

03

Inspiration

+

Take part in exciting regular intensives and workshops with international guest instructors. We frequently host world-class artists to bring the best of Bachata and other dance styles directly to Zurich.
