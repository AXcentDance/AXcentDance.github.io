What to Expect

In the Beginner 0 course, we focus on the most essential fundamentals. We understand that starting can be intimidating, so we create a supportive environment where everyone is at the same starting line.

You don't need a partner to join! Most of our students join solo.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic Steps & Time signature (The 1-2-3-4 rhythm)
  • Basic Turns & Frame
  • Simple Partner Connection
  • Weight Transfer & Posture
  • Confidence on the dance floor

Prerequisites

None! This is specifically for absolute beginners.
