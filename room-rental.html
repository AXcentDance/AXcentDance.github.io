Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rent the room for private parties? +
Yes, our studio is perfect for birthdays, workshops, and private events. Contact us for availability and customized rates for non-recurring events.
What equipment is included? +
The rental includes the high-quality sound system (Bluetooth/Aux), mirrors, and basic lighting. Additional equipment like chairs or tables can be arranged upon request.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, there is street parking available on the same street as the school. Public transport (Tram 2, Bus 31) is just 2 minutes walking distance.
