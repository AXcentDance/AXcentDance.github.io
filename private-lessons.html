FAQs

Do I need a dance partner? +
No partner required! Our instructors will dance with you, allowing you to focus purely on your own technique and leading/following skills.
Can we prepare a Wedding Dance? +
Absolutely. We specialize in creating beautiful, memorable wedding choreographies that match your song and skill level. Visit our Wedding Dance page for more details.
What should I wear? +
Comfortable clothing that allows movement. For shoes, standard dance shoes or clean indoor sneakers are best. Avoid heavy boots or flip-flops.
How much does it cost? +
We will discuss the investment personally with you. Please fill out the contact form, and we will get back to you via WhatsApp or Email.
Where do the lessons take place? +
All private lessons are held at our home base: AXcent Dance Studio (Hermetschloostrasse 73, Zurich).
