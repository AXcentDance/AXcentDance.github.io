Christmas Break & New Season 2025

As the year comes to a close, we want to thank all of our students for an amazing year of dance, connection, and growth. The studio will be taking a short break to recharge and get ready for an even more exciting 2025!

Studio Break Dates

  • Closed: December 22 - January 11
  • Re-opening: Monday, January 12

We wish you all a Merry Christmas and a joyous holiday season filled with love, laughter, and of course, dancing. Whether you are traveling or staying in Zurich, we hope you find time to rest and enjoy the festivities.

We can't wait to see you back on the dance floor in 2025 with new classes, new energy, and the same unbeatable AXcent vibes.
