Top 3 Bachata Hits Heating Up Zurich This December

Winter has officially arrived in Zurich, but inside the studio, things are heating up! The end of 2025 has brought us some incredible musical gifts, and we can't wait to share them with you. Whether you're a beginner finding your rhythm or an advanced dancer looking for musicality challenges, these tracks are essential listening.

Music is the heartbeat of what we do. Understanding the songs, feeling the breaks, and connecting with the emotion is just as important as the steps. Here are the top 3 tracks dominating our playlists right now.

1. The Titans Unite

"Inmortal" (2025 Remix)
Romeo Santos & Prince Royce

It finally happened. The collaborative album "Better Late Than Never" dropped this week, and the dance floor hasn't been the same since. While every track is a banger, "Inmortal" stands out for its classic Aventura vibes mixed with Prince Royce's smooth romanticism. It has a clear, steady beat that is perfect for practicing your basic step, but enough dramatic pauses to let you play with styling.

2. The Fresh Vibe

"Ivonny Bonita"
Karol G

Karol G continues to prove she can conquer any genre. Her new Bachata track from the "Tropicoqueta" album brings a feminine, powerful energy that we absolutely love. The tempo is slightly faster, making it great for practicing your footwork speed and turns. Ladies, this is your anthem for Lady Styling class!

3. The Local Favorite

"No Money (Bachata Edition)"
EL CROK & Cristhian Collado

Released just a few days ago, this track is already making waves in the European scene. It has that gritty, authentic Dominican flow but with a modern production polish. It's playful, fun, and impossible not to move to. We'll be using this one in our intermediate classes to work on musicality and syncopations.

Listen & Practice

We've added all these tracks to our "AXcent Practice" playlist on Spotify. Remember, the best way to improve your dancing is to listen to the music even when you aren't dancing. Let the rhythm become part of you.

Ready to move?

Come feel the music with us! Join our classes this week and put these songs to the test.
