Top Hobbies to Meet People in Zurich: Your Social Guide

Zurich is a bustling city, but finding the right hobbies to meet people can sometimes feel like a challenge. If you are looking for social activities in Zurich that are vibrant, international, and incredibly welcoming, you have come to the right place. It is the perfect time to start a new hobby that not only keeps you active but also helps you meet new friends and expand your social circle.

No Partner? No Problem!

A common misconception is that you need a partner to start dancing. At AXcent Dance, everyone comes alone. We rotate partners frequently during class, which is the secret to why our community is so tight-knit. It is one of the most effective solo hobbies in Zurich to meet people without the pressure of a traditional social setting. Whether you are looking for hobbies for singles or just want to expand your network, Bachata is the answer.

If you are looking for a beginner guide on how to start, we make it incredibly easy. You just show up, and we take care of the rest. You will be surprised at how quickly you will start recognizing people and feeling like part of the family.
