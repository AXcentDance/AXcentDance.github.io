The Complete Latin Dance School Experience

Many students come to us looking specifically for Bachata classes. However, what they discover is a comprehensive Latin Dance School that offers much more. To truly master the art of movement, we believe a dancer must explore the full spectrum of Latin rhythms.

At AXcent Dance in Zurich, our curriculum is designed to create versatile, confident dancers. We do not just teach steps; we teach music, culture, and connection across multiple disciplines.

Why Choose a Holistic Latin Dance School?

When you join a specialized Latin Dance School like AXcent Dance, you are not just signing up for a course; you are entering an academy of movement.

Our instructors, Ale and Xidan, integrate concepts from **Salsa, Mambo, ChaCha**, and classical technique into every **Bachata** class. Although our courses are focused on Bachata, these diverse Latin styles are part of our holistic curriculum to build complete dancers. This cross-disciplinary approach creates dancers who are adaptable, musical, and visually stunning to watch.
