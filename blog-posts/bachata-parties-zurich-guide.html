The Ultimate Guide to Bachata Parties in Zurich

Zurich is home to one of the most vibrant Bachata communities in Europe. Whether it is a Monday night craving or a weekend fever, there is always a place to connect and dance.

For many, the hardest part of starting Bachata is knowing where the party is. At AXcent Dance, we believe that social dancing is the soul of our community. It is where you put your weekly class training into practice and truly find your flow. If you are looking to start your journey, our Bachata classes in Zurich are designed to get you social-ready in no time. Here is your nightly map to the best social dance floors in Zurich.

Monday: Dancing by the Lake

Every Monday, experience the magic of dancing by the lake at Bürkliplatz Open Dancing. It is completely free and incredibly beginner-friendly. The open-air atmosphere makes it the perfect place to overcome those first-time social dance nerves.

Dancing outdoors at **Bürkliplatz** is a Zurich tradition. There is nothing like a sunset over Lake Zurich accompanied by the rhythms of the Dominican Republic. It is a social ecosystem where everyone is welcome.

Mid-Week Energy: Tuesday & Thursday

Tuesday nights belong to **Mi Bachata at Vior Club**, right in the heart of the city. It is often the most crowded weeknight, filled with energy and high-level dancers. While Thursdays are quieter, stay tuned to our community groups for spontaneous studio socials or smaller gatherings.

The Weekend: High-Energy Clubs

🔥

Friday Night @ Bananenreiferei

A multi-floor experience where the Bachata floor typically opens late into the night. It is a ritual for many dancers who enjoy both Salsa and Bachata under one roof.

💃

Club Silbando Specials

Mark your calendars for the **4th Friday** (Fiesta Bachata) and the **2nd Saturday** (Fiesta Pasion) of every month. These events feature workshops by local teachers followed by intense social dancing on dedicated floors.

If you are feeling nervous about your first social, do not worry. Read our Beginner Guide to learn about dance etiquette and what to expect. We often join these parties as a group, so you will always see a familiar face from the AXcent family.
