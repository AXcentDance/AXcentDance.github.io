What to Expect at the Dominican Bachata Bootcamp with Aitor & Sarah

Zurich, are you ready for the real flavor? We are proud to announce the biggest event of the year for fans of authentic dance: the Dominican Bachata Bootcamp in Zurich. On October 3rd & 4th, 2026, we are bringing two of the world's most exciting artists to AXcent Dance: Aitor Gomez and Sarah La Morena.

Sometimes you need to go back to the roots to find what can bring you to the next level. Dominican Bachata is not just about footwork; it is about musicality, flow, timing, creativity, and that unmistakable groove that makes you want to move. Explore the full history and roots of Bachata to understand the culture behind the movement. And there is nobody better to teach this than Aitor and Sarah.

Why Bootcamp Format?

At AXcent Dance, we believe in Deep Learning. Congresses are great for parties, but if you want to actually improve, you need focused time with the instructor. With 7 hours of training, you will have time to master the material. New to Bachata? Start with our beginner guide and check our weekly schedule to build your foundation before the bootcamp:

  • Understand the fundamental body movement (not just steps).
  • Work on musicality and timing.
  • Ask questions and get personal feedback.
  • Practice the material immediately.
