Mejor Tarde Que Nunca Tour 2026: Dates, Locations & Details

The wait is finally over. After shaking the world with their surprise collaborative album "Better Late Than Never" — which we reviewed in our album breakdown article — Romeo Santos and Prince Royce have officially announced their joint world tour. This is a historic moment for Bachata, bringing together the two biggest icons of the genre on one stage.

European Leg: Summer 2026

Great news for fans in Europe! The Kings are crossing the Atlantic this summer. While the full schedule is still rolling out, the following dates have been officially confirmed:

  • June 25, 2026 Seville, Spain
    Plaza de España
  • July 10, 2026 Milan, Italy
    San Siro Stadium (TBC)
  • July 19, 2026 Paris, France
    Accor Arena
  • TBA Palma de Mallorca, Spain
    Summer Festival

What About Zurich?

Current Status: Unconfirmed
While rumors are circulating about a potential Swiss date in early July (possibly July 3rd), no official announcement has been made for Zurich yet. In the meantime, keep dancing with us — explore our upcoming events and get concert-ready with our beginner guide.

US Tour Dates (Spring 2026)

Before heading to Europe, the duo will be touring across the United States. Key stops include:

  • April 11: Baltimore, MD
  • April 17: Boston, MA (TD Garden)
  • April 25: Miami, FL (Kaseya Center)
  • May 7: Houston, TX
  • May 21: Los Angeles, CA (Crypto.com Arena)
