Teach Yoga, Salsa or Pilates in Zurich

Do you want to teach Yoga, Pilates, Salsa, Kizomba, Aerobics, Reggaeton, Afro dance, mobility, stretching, fitness, or another solo movement class in Zurich? AXcent Dance is open to meeting instructors who are looking for a beautiful, professional studio and a community-minded place to grow their classes.

Our studio in Zurich Altstetten is already known for Bachata, Salsa, social dancing, workshops, and international events. At the same time, the room is also ideal for wellness classes, fitness classes, dance training, personal development workshops, and regular weekly group classes. If you are searching for a studio to rent in Zurich or a place to teach with care, please get in contact with us.

Who this is for

We are especially interested in teachers who want to offer regular or semi-regular classes in Zurich. This can include Yoga, Pilates, Aerobics, Barre, mobility, stretching, fitness, Salsa shines, Salsa styling, Kizomba footwork, Kizomba styling, Reggaeton, Afro, contemporary dance, jazz, heels, dance fitness, Latin fusion, body movement, meditation, or other high-quality movement formats.

  • Kizomba solo technique, Kizomba footwork, Kizomba styling, Urban Kiz foundations, Tarraxo body movement, musicality classes, and connection technique
  • Reggaeton classes, Afro dance, Afrobeat, Dancehall, commercial dance, heels, jazz funk, contemporary, modern dance, choreography classes, and performance training
  • Hip Hop, House dance, Waacking, Voguing, Twerk classes, Latin fusion, body movement, rhythm training, adult beginner dance classes, and private group workshops

We choose teachers with care

AXcent Dance is not just a room with mirrors. It is a community. For that reason, we are thoughtful about the teachers we collaborate with. We want every class in our space to feel professional, respectful, welcoming, and genuinely valuable for the people who attend.

We always aim to provide the best instruction possible. That does not only mean strong technique. It also means clear communication, preparation, patience, safety, kindness, and the ability to guide people with confidence without ego.

The mentality we are looking for

We would love to hear from instructors who believe in quality over shortcuts, community over ego, and consistency over hype. The right teacher for AXcent Dance cares about students as people, creates a safe learning environment, arrives prepared, respects shared spaces, and wants to grow together with the community.

If your goal is only to rent a room once and disappear, our space can still work for selected projects. However, our strongest interest is in people who want to build something with intention: a regular Yoga class, a Pilates course, a Salsa solo class, a Kizomba technique class, a Reggaeton class, an Afro dance session, a fitness format, or another concept that brings real value to Zurich.
