We Are Moving! New Studio in Zurich Altstetten

Big news! AXcent Dance is moving to a brand new location to start 2025 with a bang. We are thrilled to welcome you to our new home, a beautiful space designed to host all our activities and classes.

New Location Details

  • Address: Hermetschloostrasse 73, 8048 Zurich Altstetten (1st Floor)
  • Space: 138m² room
  • Opening: January 12, 2025

Our new studio features a 138 square meter room, with ample space for social dancing, workshops, and our regular classes. It is located on the 1st floor at Hermetschloostrasse 73.

The new location allows us to expand our offerings and provide an even better experience for our students. We have been working hard to prepare this space and can't wait to share it with you.

Come Visit Us!

We open our doors on January 12th. Check the schedule to see our first classes in the new studio can't wait to see you there!
