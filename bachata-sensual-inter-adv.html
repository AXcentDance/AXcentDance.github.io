Bachata Sensual Inter/Adv

For experienced dancers who want to push their limits. We explore intricate patterns, advanced musicality concepts, and high-level technique.

Thursdays 20:30
Location
AXcent Dance Studio

What to Expect

This is our highest level class. We assume you have a solid foundation in Bachata Sensual and are ready for a challenge. The pace is faster, and we focus on detail, precision, and creativity.

We will work on complex figures, but always with a focus on lead/follow technique and safety.

What You Will Learn

  • Advanced Turn Patterns
  • Complex Body Isolations
  • Musical Interpretation
  • Dips & Tricks (Social Safe)
  • Improvisation Tools

Prerequisites

Completion of Improver level or at least 2 years of regular dancing. You must be proficient in body movement, isolations, and complex timing.
