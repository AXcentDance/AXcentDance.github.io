IMPROVER

Bachata Sensual Improver

Take your dancing to the next level. This course is for dancers who know the basics and want to refine their technique, expand their repertoire, and dance with more confidence.

Day & Time
Wednesdays 20:30
Location
AXcent Dance Studio
Level
1 - 2 Years

What to Expect

In the Improver level, we move beyond the basics and start exploring more complex combinations and musical interpretation. We focus heavily on the quality of movement and connection.

You will learn how to combine different elements fluently and how to adapt your dancing to different songs and partners.

What You Will Learn

  • Complex Turn Patterns
  • Advanced Body Isolations
  • Musicality & Breaks
  • Leading/Following Dynamics
  • Social Dance Etiquette

Prerequisites

Completion of Sensual Foundation or at least 1 year of regular dancing. You should be comfortable with body waves and basic isolations.
