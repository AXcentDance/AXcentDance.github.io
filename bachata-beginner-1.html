LEVEL 1

Bachata Beginner 1: Master the Foundation

The essential foundation for your Bachata journey. This course is for dancers with light experience who want to move beyond the basics, master the foundation of the dance, and build a solid start for being ready to social dance.

Day & Time
Tuesdays 19:30
Location
AXcent Dance Studio
Level
3 - 6 months

What to Expect

This course is designed for dancers with 3 to 6 months of experience. If you want to improve your Bachata in Zurich and master the foundation of the dance to be ready for social dancing, this is the perfect level for you.

You do not need a partner to join! We rotate partners during class, which is the best way to learn leading and following (and to make new friends while staying active!).

What You Will Learn

  • Essential Leading and Following for Social Dance
  • Foundational body movements: Waves, hips, and chest
  • Solid foundations for a confident start
  • Partner connection and floor awareness
  • Dynamic flow and rhythmic understanding

Prerequisites

Approx. 3-6 months of Bachata experience.

If you are coming from a long day at work, please bring a change of shirt, deodorant, and mints.
